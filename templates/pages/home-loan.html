Home Loan Eligibility

To qualify for a home loan, you should meet the following basic criteria:

For Salaried Individuals:
  • Age: 21 to 65 years
  • Minimum monthly income: ₹25,000
  • Employment: 2+ years of work experience
  • Credit score: 750 or above
For Self-Employed:
  • Age: 21 to 65 years
  • Business vintage: 3+ years
  • Annual income: ₹3 lakhs or above
  • Credit score: 750 or above

Required Documents

Identity & Address Proof:
  • PAN Card (mandatory)
  • Aadhaar Card
  • Passport / Driving License / Voter ID
  • Recent utility bills (for address proof)
Income Proof:
  • Last 6 months' salary slips
  • Last 2 years' ITR with computation
  • Form 16 (for salaried)
  • Bank statements (last 6 months)
